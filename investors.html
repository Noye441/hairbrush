A consumer-focused luxury haircare brand building UppieBrush for curly and textured hair.

Company Overview

Alpha Uppies is developing UppieBrush, a premium brush created to address common pain points in textured hair care, including frizz, breakage, painful brushing, and detangling difficulty.

Prototype Progress

The company is currently focused on prototype development, product refinement, brand identity, and consumer positioning.

Future Growth Plan

Phase 1

Complete the product prototype and begin early feedback testing.

Phase 2

Build digital brand awareness and grow direct-to-consumer interest.

Phase 3

Expand into broader textured-hair tools and premium beauty accessories.
