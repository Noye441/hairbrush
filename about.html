About Alpha Uppies

Our Story

Alpha Uppies was created to design better tools for people with curly and textured hair. We saw how traditional brushes often lead to frizz, breakage, pain, and difficult detangling, and we wanted to create a more thoughtful, luxurious experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create luxury hair tools that make curl care easier, gentler, and more beautiful without sacrificing comfort, quality, or style.
